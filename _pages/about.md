# About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at MIT EECS advised by Prof. <a href="https://yilundu.github.io/">Yilun Du</a>. I received my M.S. degree in MIT EECS in 2026, and B.E. degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2024. During the undergraduate period, I was very fortunate to work with Prof. <a href="http://oa.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/dailinglong/" style="text-decoration: none;">Linglong Dai</a> on wireless communication systems, and Prof. <a href="https://tianfan.info/" style="text-decoration: none;">Tianfan Xue</a> on NeRF and 3D vision. 

My research interests include 3D vision, robotics, and generative world models. I am interested in exploring generative modeling to reason, predict, and interact with the physical world.
